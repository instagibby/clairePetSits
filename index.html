My name is Claire Godfrey and even though Claire Pet Sits was officially established in 2017, my experience with animals began about 15 years prior.

I attended Iowa State University for Animal Science. Though I found it to be a bit too much about farm-type animals, I still learned invaluable information about the animal kingdom. I have been a volunteer with Best Friends Animal Society, the country's largest non profit, no kill animal shelter. I also worked for a small local non profit, no kill animal shelter. I found I enjoy working with animals more one-on-one, so I left to be my own boss.

With a small zoo of our own, having the opportunity to meet, play with, and grow to love a myriad of animals in the metro area is such a joy.

Whether your non-human family member has fur, feathers, or scales, I would love to spend my days getting to know them!
